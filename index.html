AboutMe

Web Developer and Designer

I am Mehrnaz Jahandideh, 26 years old, and I come from Iran. My greatest passion is creating – whether in design and drawing or in web development and building websites. For me, design and programming are not separate fields, but different forms of the same creative process. After my studies, I moved professionally towards technology. I initially worked in CRM, but quickly realized that this path did not match my true passion for creative work. Therefore, I consciously decided to seriously pursue programming. I started with Python and then expanded my knowledge in Django, HTML, and CSS while developing my own web projects. My goal is to build a bridge between art and technology – so that digital products are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and intuitive, creating a complete user experience.
